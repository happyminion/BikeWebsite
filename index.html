Design

A brand new bicycle concept was designed to bring into light a beautiful, minimalistic design. Unique and powerful structure will stand out and reflect your stylish personality anywhere you go.

Features

Foldable structure allows for minimum storage, letting you take your bike arywhere you go. With up to 9 gears, an ultra-light body and innovative wheel technology, BikeX weights only 12 pounds and with just a little twist it can turn from a modern city bike to a comfortable transport for a long-distance trips.

Customize

Let your personality shine through! With 5 colors to choose from, you can have your bike to be white, black, red, green or gold. It is also possible to adjust the amount of gears: 3, 5 or 9. Moreover, we have two different models designed specifically for men and women.
